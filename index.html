About EmpRoster

The scope of the project is to develop a smart scheduling system for efficient workforce management. The software helps organize and optimize employee schedules, manage shift assignments, and handle time-off requests. It streamlines attendance tracking, automates schedule creation, and ensures optimal staff coverage while considering employee preferences and labor regulations.
