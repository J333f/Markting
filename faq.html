Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

? What is EmpRoster?

EmpRoster is a smart, cloud-based workforce management platform designed to help businesses efficiently schedule shifts, track employee attendance, and streamline workforce operations.

? Who can use EmpRoster?

EmpRoster is designed for businesses of all sizes, including retail stores, restaurants, healthcare providers, and corporate offices that need to manage employee schedules.

? Is EmpRoster available on mobile devices?

Yes! EmpRoster is accessible on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, ensuring you can manage schedules and track attendance anytime, anywhere.

Features & Functionality

? How does the shift scheduling feature work?

Our intuitive scheduling system allows managers to create, edit, and publish shifts in real-time. Employees can also view their schedules, swap shifts (with manager approval), and receive updates.

? Does EmpRoster support real-time notifications?

Absolutely! Employees and managers receive instant notifications for shift updates, approvals, reminders, and urgent changes.

? Can I integrate EmpRoster with other tools?

Yes, EmpRoster supports integration with payroll systems, communication tools, and third-party HR platforms.

Pricing & Subscription

? Is EmpRoster free?

EmpRoster offers a free trial with basic features. We have different subscription plans depending on your business size and needs.

? What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for monthly or yearly subscriptions.

? Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. However, refunds are subject to our cancellation policy.

Support & Assistance

? How can I get support if I have an issue?

You can reach out to our support team via email, live chat, or phone. We also have a help center with tutorials and guides.
